Detailed Description

User's DS settings.

Defines the structure the DS firmware uses for transfer of the user's settings to the booted program.

Theme/Color values:

  • 0 = Gray
  • 1 = Brown
  • 2 = Red
  • 3 = Pink
  • 4 = Orange
  • 5 = Yellow
  • 6 = Yellow/Green-ish
  • 7 = Green
  • 8 = Dark Green
  • 9 = Green/Blue-ish
  • 10 = Light Blue
  • 11 = Blue
  • 12 = Dark Blue
  • 13 = Dark Purple
  • 14 = Purple
  • 15 = Purple/Red-ish

Language values:

  • 0 = Japanese
  • 1 = English
  • 2 = French
  • 3 = German
  • 4 = Italian
  • 5 = Spanish
  • 6 = Chinese(?)
  • 7 = Unknown/Reserved
